About

The "Crisismap" application provides users with aggregated information around crisis. We integrate news from media and data from sensors like Earth observation and meteo models. We consider it’s important to know what is going on around you and nearby, regarding crisis situations and media’s attention to the particular cases.

Tools

  • map of the realtime events
  • newsline
  • search by history date
  • crisis alerts (under development)

About technology

News feeds are aggregating automaticaly by certain keywords (e.g. “wildfires”) and parsing to get additional context on geolocation and emergency. “News on the map” uses machine learning technology to improve the accuracy of the results. Data feeds:
  • Wildfire information based on satellites monitoring: SCANEX, NASA, SSEC Wisconsin
  • Weather data and models: Openweathermap
